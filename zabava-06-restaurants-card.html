Imperial Café

4.7
170-300
Ljubljana, Sretensky b-r, 4
+386 0 36 20 07 23
Book a table
Add to FavoritesIn Favorites
A chamber restaurant that has been operating on Sretensky Boulevard for more than 15 years, with classics of Italian cuisine and author’s recipes from the chef, who can often be found in the hall chatting with guests or cooking pasta in a cheese head. Special attention is paid to the quality of products: ingredients are delivered from the Apennine Peninsula, cheeses are ordered from farmers, tomato sauce is cooked according to a secret recipe. Generous portions of pasta (15 types!) in osteria, they are served in plates made by hand from the famous Italian ceramics from Umbria. Attention to detail can be traced not only in the elegant serving, but also in the interior — the oven is decorated with handmade tiles, the ceilings are painted in Florentine style, chandeliers and Murano glass balls that were ordered in Venice
